MY FAVORITE HTML TAGS

Introduction. HTML, or Hypertext Markup Language,
has a variety of different tags that assist with the structure of
web pages; however, I will discuss my three favorite tags and showcase
their respective utility.

<svg> . SVG, or Scalable Vector Graphics, is used
to draw and define shapes, graphic images and more.
It does not require resolution dependency which supports
it's scalable factor.

<img> . Sending and receiving images
has been a staple for the World Wide Web. In fact, the organization (CERN)
that launched WWW also uploaded the first image.
And here it is, in all of it's primitive Adobe Photoshop glory:

<iframe> . Embedding is a feature that allows an external application
to be placed on to a web page. The iframe tag acts as a container
for various plug-ins or interactive media that may be inserted in the container.
Youtube offers an embed option with most (all?) of its videos and supplies the code
to embed the video to a web page.
