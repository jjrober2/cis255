Wordpress VS Bootstrap

Versus is a little deceiving, because Wordpress and Boostrap are completely different animals. Wordpress is more intuitive for anyone who is just starting out with web design. For example, manipulatiing objects in windows, or customizing preferences by clicking buttons and getting immediate feedback can be more helpful for someone who is just starting out.

Bootstrap however gives you all of the necessary tools, and then some. You are able to manipulate the tinest details with Bootstrap, that I feel you don't get the same control with wordpress. I am able to create my own container, or use an existing one, and insert any custimization of code and have it look and feel responsive in a way that matches or exceeds Wordpress.
